About

Built for those who
require more than consensus.

Mayacama Research was founded on a single conviction: that the most durable alpha in markets is not discovered — it is engineered. Through first-principles quantitative research and proprietary simulation infrastructure, we develop the tools that let institutional allocators think and act with genuine precision.

Our work sits at the intersection of financial theory, applied mathematics, and computational design. We are not a fund. We are not a data vendor. We are the analytical architecture that powers what comes next.

Engagement with Mayacama Research is by introduction only. We work with a select group of hedge funds, family offices, and institutional allocators for whom the standard toolkit is insufficient.
